Our Journey

The Effectical Story

Effectical Service Solutions was started because we think businesses in India should get digital products that are made with a purpose. We do not just put things together from templates and shortcuts.

We began as a team that was really good at making websites. Over time we got better at designing portals, coming up with plans and running platforms.

One big thing we did was make our portals — Elite Bandhan, NikahWed, Sangam Vivah and Digiwanna. Running these portals was a learning experience. It taught us what our clients go through every day. This experience helps us make things for other people.

Now Effectical Service Solutions is a limited company that is officially registered and growing. We have a team that is getting bigger, a list of projects delivered across many industries, and a way of working that is practical, direct, and focused on what actually gets results for our clients.
