About Our Services

What services does Effectical Service Solutions provide?
We provide a range of digital services including website development, custom web portals, matrimonial platform development, business website design, SEO-friendly development, UI/UX design, website maintenance and support, and digital growth solutions. We are a full-service digital solutions company serving businesses across India.
Do you build custom websites or only use templates?
We build custom websites and web portals for our clients. We do not use generic website builders or pre-made templates for client projects. Every solution is developed according to the specific requirements, design preferences, and business goals of the client.
Can you build a matrimonial or matchmaking website?
Yes — this is an area where we have direct, hands-on experience. We operate three matrimonial portals ourselves: Elite Bandhan, NikahWed, and Sangam Vivah. We understand exactly what features a matrimonial platform needs — profile management, search and filters, membership plans, privacy controls, admin panel, and more. We can build a fully functional matrimonial platform tailored to your community or target audience.
Do you provide website maintenance and support after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and support services. This covers bug fixes, content updates, security patches, performance checks, and general upkeep. We recommend a maintenance plan to all clients after launch to ensure their website or portal continues to function smoothly over time.
Can you redesign an existing website?
Yes. We take on website redesign projects regularly. Whether your current website looks outdated, is not mobile-friendly, or simply doesn't reflect your brand well — we can redesign it into a modern, professional website. We typically review your existing site, understand your current pain points, and propose a new design direction that aligns with your goals.
Do you build SEO-ready websites?
All websites we build include SEO fundamentals as a standard — proper heading structure, meta tags, clean and semantic HTML, alt text for images, fast page load performance, and mobile-friendliness. These are core web development practices we follow on every project. For advanced SEO work like content strategy and ongoing optimization, we can discuss that separately based on your goals.

Process & Pricing

How does the project process work?
Our process is straightforward. First, we have a discovery conversation where we understand your requirements in detail. Then we prepare a proposal covering the scope, timeline, and budget. Once agreed, we proceed with design, then development, followed by testing and client review. Finally, we launch and provide post-launch support. You'll receive regular updates throughout the project.
How long does it take to complete a website project?
Timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. A custom web portal or complex platform can take 6–12 weeks or more depending on the scope. We always provide a clear timeline estimate before starting, and we stick to agreed deadlines.
How much does a website or portal cost?
Pricing depends on the specific requirements of your project — the number of pages, features required, design complexity, and timeline. We don't have fixed pricing packages because every project is different. We provide a detailed quote after understanding your requirements. There are no hidden charges — what we quote is what you pay.
Do you require full payment upfront?
No. We typically follow a milestone-based payment structure — a portion upfront to start the project, another portion at a key milestone (like design approval), and the final payment before or at delivery. This keeps things fair and transparent for both sides. Exact payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before the project begins.

Technical & Support

What technologies do you use to build websites?
We use a range of web technologies based on project requirements. For frontend, we use HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. For backend and dynamic portals, we primarily use PHP with MySQL databases. We also work with CMS platforms where appropriate. Our selection of technology is always based on what's most suitable for the project — not just what's trendy.
Will my website work on mobile devices?
Yes. All websites we build are fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes. Mobile-friendliness is not optional — it is a standard requirement in everything we build.
How can we contact your team for support?
You can reach us via our contact page, by email at info@effectical.in, or by phone during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–6 PM IST). For existing clients, we typically set up a dedicated communication channel during the project for faster responses.
Do you provide hosting and domain services?
We can assist clients in selecting suitable hosting providers and setting up their hosting environment. We also help with domain registration guidance. While we don't run our own hosting service, we provide recommendations and handle the technical setup as part of project delivery where required.
