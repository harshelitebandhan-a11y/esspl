Who We Are

Effectical Service Solutions Private Limited

Effectical Service Solutions Private Limited is a digital services and web development company registered in India. We specialize in building practical, well-designed, and performance-oriented digital solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our work spans from clean corporate websites and complex web portals to fully functional matrimonial platforms. We don't just write code — we understand business goals and build digital products that contribute to real growth.

What sets us apart is that we operate multiple live platforms ourselves. This real-world experience makes us better developers, better designers, and better partners for our clients.

Our Journey

The Effectical Story

Effectical Service Solutions was founded with a straightforward belief: that businesses in India deserve quality digital products built with real intent — not assembled from templates and shortcuts.

Starting as a small team with a strong focus on web development, we gradually grew our capabilities across design, portal development, digital strategy, and platform operations.

One of our defining moments was building and launching our own portals — Elite Bandhan, NikahWed, Sangam Vivah, and Digiwanna. Running live platforms taught us what clients face every day. That experience directly improves the solutions we build for others.

Today, we are a registered private limited company with a growing team, a portfolio of successful projects, and a strong commitment to building digital solutions that make a practical difference.
